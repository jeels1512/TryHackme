**EDA is important**

Understand dataset structure 

Find Patterns

Detect errors

Choose features

Pick models


**There are three types of EDA**

**1. Univariate**

You are looking at just one column.

example: Age distribution

- What you check:
  Is it normal or skewed?
  Are there weird values?

- Tools:
  Histogram
  Box plot

**2. Bivariate**

Do these two things relate?

# Example: 
 - study hours vs marks
 - Salary vs experience

# Tools:
- Scatter plot
- Correlation


**3. Maultivariate**

Multiple variables interacting together

# Example:
 Salary based on age + education + experience
